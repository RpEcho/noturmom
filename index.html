Download Solutions

The app isn't uploaded on Google Playstore, so Google Protect blocks the app from getting installed. To fix this problem:

  1. Go to Google Playstore.
  2. Disable Google Play Protect.
  3. After doing that, download the app.
  4. After downloading it and installing it, you can enable Google Play Protect again.
